CONSTITUENT UNIT'S LOGOS

The development of new marks (including logos) by Constituent Units, partnership, subsidiaries and collaborative campus(es) of HKUST shall comply with the respective Policy & Guidelines on the Use of the University’s Titles, Names and Logos of HKUST and HKUST(GZ).

Constituent Units of HKUST are more specifically defined in the said respective Policy & Guidelines, but in general refer to Schools/Hubs, offices, laboratories, centers, institutes, PRC representative offices of the University and other official units established by the University, as the case may be.

To obtain approval for a newly created logo, constituent units are required to submit a formal request via the New Marks Application Form. A user tutorial video can be found here.

CONSTITUENT UNIT'S LOGOS

To maintain brand consistency among the University and University’s Constituent Units, the identity or name of the University should be featured as part of the design of the Constituent Units logo. They should follow the below guidelines and feature them in the corresponding language version.

The standard University combination logo should be placed on the left side while the graphic brand identity, logotype, and/or name of the Constituent Units should be placed on the right side and share the same spacing rules and language order as the standard University combination logo. The graphic brand identity, logotype, and/or name of the Constituent Units should not exceed the height of the University combination logo. STANDARD TYPEFACE FOR CONSTITUENT UNIT'S LOGO

The Constituent Unit’s logo should follow the Standard University Combination Logo Typeface:

ENGLISH: Futura Medium

Traditional Chinese: 黑體

Simplified Chinese: 思源黑体 – 简体中文版

If a Constituent Unit’s logo consists of the full name or abbreviated name of the University, the typeface of the University name should be in Futura Medium for English, 黑體 for Traditional Chinese and 思源黑体 – 简体中文版 for Simplified Chinese.

成員單位標誌

為大學成員單位、合作項目、附屬機構及協作校園設計新標記（包括標誌）時，需要遵從香港科大和港科大（廣州）關於 「使用大學名銜、稱謂和標誌」 的相關政策和指引。

在上述的政策及指引中，對於香港科大的「成員單位」有較具體的定義，通常是指學院/樞紐、辦公室或事務處、實驗室、中心、學研機構、大學駐中國內地辦公室及其他由大學設立的官方機構，視乎具體情況而定。

如要為新標誌申請批核，各成員單位必須通過《新標誌申請表》提交正式請求。按此觀看教學影片。

成员单位标志

为大学成员单位、合作项目、附属机构及协作校园设计新标记（包括标志）时，需要遵从香港科大和港科大（广州）关于 “使用大学名衔、称谓和标志”的相关政策和指引。

在上述的政策及指引中，对于香港科大的“成员单位”有较具体的定义，通常是指学院/枢纽、办公室或事务处、实验室、中心、学研机构、大学驻中国内地办公室及其他由大学设立的官方机构，视乎具体情况而定。

如要为新标志申请批核，各成员单位必须通过《新标志申请表》提交正式请求。按此观看教学影片。

成员单位标志

为了保持大学和大学成员单位之间的品牌一致性，大学的识别或名称应作为成员单位标志设计的一部分。设计应遵从以下准则，并体现在对应的语言版本中。

标准的大学复合式标志应置于左侧，而成员单位的品牌图象识别、文字标志和/或名称应置于右侧，并与标准的大学复合式标志遵从相同的间距规则及语言顺序。成员单位的品牌图象识别、文字标志和/或名称的高度不得超过大学的复合式标志的高度。关于规格大小、间距和最小留白范围的指引，请参考以下例子。

成员单位标志的标准字体
