THE RED BIRD SUNDIAL SCULPTURE

The Red Bird Sundial is the most prominent brand icon of HKUST.

This section provides the guidelines for replicating The Red Bird Sundial in 3-D, for upholding the integrity of this important brand property of the University, including but not limited to any 3-D installation display, production of trophy and souvenirs, etc.

USAGE

The Red Bird Sundial was created to convey specific meaning and messages. Regardless of the size and material, any replication of The Red Bird Sundial should follow stringent measurements in all dimensions to retain the spirit and essence of this brand icon from any angle.

There is no restriction on the size for 3-D replicas of The Red Bird Sundial, provided that all proportions can be maintained and truly represented without any distortion.

The Red Bird Sundial should also be presented in its entirety with no alterations or the intrusion of any other external objects. This rule applies to all versions of the Red Bird Sundial. Otherwise, the production of the altered 3-D replicas should be reviewed by Global Engagement & Communications Office (GECO) and approved by the Vice-President for Institutional Advancement to ensure it will not adversely affect the image of The Red Bird Sundial.

應用指引

紅鳥日晷的創建是為傳達特定意思和信息。不論尺寸及物料如何，任何紅鳥日晷的複製，應在各維度上遵從嚴謹的尺寸規定，以確保從任何角度都能保持這個品牌標誌的精神和精髓。

紅鳥日晷的 3D 複製沒有尺寸限制，前提是所有比例均保持一致，並真實呈現且沒有變形。

紅鳥日晷必須完整呈現，不可改動或加入任何其他外部物體。此規定適用於所有版本的紅鳥日晷。否則，經修改的 3D 複製必須經環球事務及傳訊處（GECO）審核，並由副校長（大學拓展）批准，以確保不會對紅鳥日晷的形象造成不良影響。

使用指南

红鸟日晷的创建是为了传达特定的含义和信息。不管尺寸和材质如何，任何红鸟日晷的复制品都应在各个维度上遵从严谨的图形比例，以从任何角度都能最好地表达这个品牌标志的精神和精髓。

红鸟日晷的3D复制品没有尺寸限制，前提是所有比例都保持一致并真实呈现图形而不会出现任何失真。

红鸟日晷也应完整呈现，不得改动或加入任何其他外部物体。此规则适用于所有版本的红鸟日晷。否则，修改后的3D复制品应由环球事务及传讯处（GECO）审查并由副校长（大学拓展）作常规审核及批准，以确保不会对红鸟日晷的形象产生不利影响。
